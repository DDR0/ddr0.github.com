CUBE TRAINS

Solve routing puzzles in the inner city with Cube Trains. You start off as a young urban planner trying to connect a city's fragmented rail lines together, and work your way up to become an old urban planner trying to connect a city's fragmented rail lines together! Over the course of your life, you'll build towering structures, beautiful bridges, and some truly convoluted infrastructure. Download it below.

Features:

  • Puzzles in three dimensions.
  • Twenty reasonably unique levels.
  • Infinite undo/redo.
  • A cheering crowd, just for you.
  • Colourblind mode.

Coming summer 2012. Yours for the price of one Canadian dollar.
