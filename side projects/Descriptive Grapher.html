This page requires a modern browser with javascript enabled. Firefox or Chrome should work out-of-the-box.
Mouse: Move Scale

Drag functions around with the mouse. Click on the equation to the side to edit. Looks to ab/cd for offset/scale values.

The Descriptive Grapher is a graph tool for those who hate graphing. It gives you a formula from a graph as well as the graph from the formula, because you need both.
